Rule ID: CA1-0010
Field: Common Equity Tier 1 Capital
Row/Column: R010, C010
Instruction: Includes capital instruments that meet Article 26(1). Typically common shares issued by the institution that are fully paid up.

Rule ID: CA1-0020
Field: Retained Earnings
Row/Column: R130, C010
Instruction: Profits that the bank has kept for its own use rather than distributing them to shareholders as dividends.

Rule ID: CA1-0030
Field: Accumulated other comprehensive income
Row/Column: R180, C010
Instruction: Includes unrealised gains and losses on financial assets measured at fair value through OCI.

Rule ID: CA1-0040
Field: Additional Tier 1 instruments
Row/Column: R020, C010
Instruction: Capital instruments that meet the conditions in Article 52(1) of CRR. Must be perpetual, subordinated, and loss-absorbing.

Rule ID: CA1-0050
Field: Share premium accounts related to CET1
Row/Column: R120, C010
Instruction: Premium paid above par value for common equity shares. Must relate to instruments included in CET1 capital.

Rule ID: CA1-0060
Field: Minority interests in CET1
Row/Column: R200, C010
Instruction: CET1 capital of consolidated subsidiaries attributable to third parties. Subject to recognition limits under Article 84 CRR.

Rule ID: CA1-0070
Field: Tier 2 Capital
Row/Column: R030, C010
Instruction: Capital instruments meeting Article 63 CRR conditions. Includes subordinated debt with minimum 5-year maturity.

Rule ID: CA1-0080
Field: Deduction: Intangible assets
Row/Column: R250, C010
Instruction: Goodwill and other intangible assets must be deducted from CET1 capital per Article 36(1)(b) CRR. Apply net of associated deferred tax liabilities.

Rule ID: CA1-0090
Field: Deduction: Deferred tax assets
Row/Column: R260, C010
Instruction: Deferred tax assets that rely on future profitability must be deducted from CET1 per Article 36(1)(c) CRR. Threshold exemptions apply.

Rule ID: CA1-0100
Field: Deduction: IRB shortfall
Row/Column: R270, C010
Instruction: Shortfall of provisions to expected losses under IRB approach. Deducted 50% from Tier 1 and 50% from Tier 2 per Article 36(1)(d).

Rule ID: CA1-0110
Field: Deduction: Securitisation positions
Row/Column: R280, C010
Instruction: Securitisation positions receiving 1250% risk weight must be deducted from capital per Article 36(1)(k) CRR.

Rule ID: CA1-0120
Field: Deduction: Holdings in financial institutions
Row/Column: R290, C010
Instruction: Significant investments in CET1 instruments of financial sector entities exceeding 10% threshold. Deducted per Article 36(1)(i) CRR.

Rule ID: CA1-0130
Field: Prudential filters: AFS reserves
Row/Column: R140, C010
Instruction: Unrealised gains on available-for-sale assets. May be subject to prudential filters per Article 35 CRR depending on institution's choice.

Rule ID: CA1-0140
Field: Transitional adjustments
Row/Column: R300, C010
Instruction: Phased-in recognition of capital instruments and deductions during CRR transitional period. Percentages defined in Part Ten CRR.

Rule ID: CA1-0150
Field: Regulatory adjustments: Cash flow hedges
Row/Column: R150, C010
Instruction: Gains and losses on cash flow hedge reserves. Treatment depends on whether institution applies IAS 39 or IFRS 9.

Rule ID: CA1-0160
Field: Regulatory adjustments: Pension fund assets
Row/Column: R310, C010
Instruction: Defined benefit pension fund assets net of liabilities. Deducted from CET1 per Article 36(1)(e) CRR.

Rule ID: CA1-0170
Field: Regulatory adjustments: Own credit risk
Row/Column: R160, C010
Instruction: Gains or losses on liabilities valued at fair value resulting from changes in own credit standing. Must be derecognised from CET1.

Rule ID: CA1-0180
Field: Capital instruments eligible for grandfathering
Row/Column: R320, C010
Instruction: Capital instruments issued before CRR implementation that do not meet new criteria. Subject to phase-out schedule per Article 483-486 CRR.

Rule ID: CA1-0190
Field: Qualifying Tier 1 capital
Row/Column: R040, C010
Instruction: Sum of CET1 and Additional Tier 1 capital after all regulatory adjustments and deductions. Used for Tier 1 capital ratio calculation.

Rule ID: CA1-0200
Field: Total Own Funds
Row/Column: R050, C010
Instruction: Sum of Tier 1 and Tier 2 capital. Represents total regulatory capital available to absorb losses per Article 72 CRR.
