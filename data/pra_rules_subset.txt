Rule ID: CA1-0010
Field: Common Equity Tier 1 Capital
Row/Column: R010, C010
Instruction: Includes capital instruments that meet Article 26(1). Typically common shares issued by the institution that are fully paid up.

Rule ID: CA1-0020
Field: Retained Earnings
Row/Column: R130, C010
Instruction: Profits that the bank has kept for its own use rather than distributing them to shareholders as dividends.

Rule ID: CA1-0030
Field: Accumulated other comprehensive income
Row/Column: R180, C010
Instruction: Includes unrealised gains and losses on financial assets measured at fair value through OCI.
